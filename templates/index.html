IPL Team Ticket Prices

Here are some of the factors that affect the price of IPL tickets:

  • Team Popularity: Matches featuring popular teams like Mumbai Indians, Chennai Super Kings, or Royal Challengers Bangalore typically command higher prices due to increased demand.
  • Match Type: Regular season matches are generally cheaper compared to playoffs and the final, with the final being the most expensive ticket.
  • Stand Location: Seats closer to the pitch, offering a premium viewing experience, come at a premium cost. Additionally, stands with amenities like shade or air conditioning might be pricier.
  • Stadium Location: Tickets in larger cities with modern stadiums tend to be more expensive compared to smaller cities.
  • Demand and Supply: Limited availability due to high demand can lead to steeper prices. Early booking is often crucial for securing affordable tickets.

Complete IPL 2025 Ticket Booking Guide

Booking IPL Tickets through BookMyShow

BookMyShow is one of the primary platforms for booking IPL tickets online. Follow these comprehensive steps to secure your tickets:

  1. Visit the BookMyShow Website or App: Access BookMyShow through their official website (www.bookmyshow.com) or download their mobile application from your device's app store. The app is available for both Android and iOS devices.
  2. Select Your Location: Choose your city from the location dropdown menu. This helps BookMyShow show you relevant matches happening in your area. You can also allow the app to detect your location automatically.
  3. Search for IPL Tickets: During the IPL season, you'll usually find IPL tickets featured on the homepage. Alternatively, use the search bar and type "IPL 2025" or navigate to the Sports category to find all available IPL matches.
  4. Select the IPL Match: Browse through the list of upcoming matches. Each listing will show the teams playing, match date, time, and venue. Click on your preferred match to view available tickets.
  5. Choose the Seating Category: Different stadiums offer various seating options:
    • VIP Boxes/Corporate Boxes (Premium Experience)
    • Pavilion/Premium Stands (Best View)
    • North/South/East/West Stands (Different Viewing Angles)
    • General Admission (Budget-Friendly Option)
  6. Select the Number of Tickets: Choose how many tickets you want to purchase. Most venues have a limit on the maximum number of tickets per transaction (usually 4-6 tickets).
  7. Review Seating Plan: Carefully examine the stadium layout to understand your seat location. The interactive seating chart shows:
    • View from different sections
    • Seat pricing for each category
    • Available and unavailable seats
    • Entry gates for different sections
  8. Login or Sign Up: You'll need a BookMyShow account to complete the purchase. Sign up using:
    • Email address
    • Mobile number
    • Social media accounts
  9. Enter Payment Details: BookMyShow offers multiple payment options:
    • Credit/Debit Cards
    • Net Banking
    • UPI
    • Mobile Wallets
    • EMI Options (for eligible transactions)
  10. Complete the Transaction: After payment:
    • You'll receive an email confirmation
    • SMS confirmation will be sent to your registered mobile
    • E-tickets will be available in your BookMyShow account
    • Download and save your tickets for match day

Important Notes:

  • Booking window usually opens 2-3 weeks before the match
  • Popular matches may sell out quickly
  • Keep your ID proof ready for verification at the stadium
  • Check stadium guidelines for prohibited items
